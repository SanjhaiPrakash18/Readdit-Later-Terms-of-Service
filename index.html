Description of Service

Core Functionality

Readdit Later is a Chrome browser extension that provides:

  • Reddit Integration: Connects to your Reddit account via secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Post Management: Retrieves, displays, and organizes your saved Reddit posts
  • Search & Filtering: Advanced search, filtering, and sorting capabilities
  • Labels & Notes: Custom labeling and note-taking for saved posts
  • Read Status Tracking: Mark posts as read/unread for better organization
